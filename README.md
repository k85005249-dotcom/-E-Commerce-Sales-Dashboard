**📊 E-Commerce Sales Dashboard**

**🧠 Key Insights & Business Observations 
👋 About This Project**

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard designed to analyze sales and performance, customer behavior, product trends, delivery efficiency, and regional demand for an e-commerce business.

The goal is to turn raw data into actionable business insights 📈.<img width="1822" height="660" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/dfdc2e5f-4b44-47bc-a331-b3f1034900e7" />



**🎯 Key Business Insights**

🛒 Preferred Sales Channel

📱 App is the most preferred sales channel, contributing the highest order volume, indicating strong customer adoption of mobile purchasing.

📦 Customer Buying Pattern

🧮 Majority of customers purchase 2–3 items per order, showing strong potential for product bundling and cross-sell strategies.

👕 Top-Selling Products

🔥 Sneakers & T-Shirts are the top-selling product categories across all genders, driving maximum revenue contribution.

🚚 Delivery Performance

⚡ Most orders are delivered within 2–3 days, reflecting efficient logistics operations and a positive customer fulfillment experience.

📍 Regional Demand Analysis

📌 Southern California generates the highest order quantity, making it a key focus region for targeted marketing and inventory planning.

💡 Business Recommendation

**📢 Recommendation:**
👉 Increase App-based marketing campaigns in Southern California and introduce product bundles for Sneakers & T-Shirts to maximize revenue and customer engagement.
